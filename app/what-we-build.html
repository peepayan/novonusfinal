What we build § 01 — 03
01

A multimodal data-capture rig.

A wearable system — EMG for muscle-level force, plus force, motion, and vision — that records how a human expert applies force during a real task, synchronized in real time. It captures the force intuition cameras and teleoperation miss.

02

A force-grounded training pipeline.

It predicts grip force from muscle signals, validated against real sensors at R² = 0.96, turns a small set of real demonstrations into thousands of physics-simulated scenarios, verifies each against the real human force so only valid ones survive, and trains a force-aware policy that handles fragile parts the way a skilled human would.

03

The deepest force dataset in fragile assembly.

Everything compounds into a proprietary, force-grounded dataset — our core moat and the engine for every robot we train.
